ABOUT US

CNA Legal : We are passionate about achieving the best possible result for you

CNA LEGAL is the top Sports and Immigration law firm of choice in Nigeria with an excellent background in Corporate & Commercial law practice. We provide efficient legal representation to a highly diversified clientele including Individuals, International business executives, private and public companies, banks and investors.

With over 17 years experience, ours is a full-service firm providing quality legal services on all aspects of Nigerian Immigration law; visas for business and investors, sports and entertainment law. As specialist lawyers in Corporate and Commercial law we will provide you with the legal know how on setting up and doing business in Nigeria with minimal hassles, avoiding unnecessary bureaucratic red tape in an efficient and cost effective manner.
